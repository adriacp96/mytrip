# MyTrip - Travel Planning Made Simple

Welcome to MyTrip! This app makes it easy to plan group trips, track expenses, and organize everything you need for your adventure.

## What is MyTrip?

MyTrip is your digital travel companion that helps you:
- **Plan your itinerary** - Add activities, sightseeing spots, and important notes for each day
- **Split expenses fairly** - Keep track of who paid for what and who owes whom
- **Create packing lists** - Never forget anything again
- **Stay organized** - Everything in one place, accessible anywhere

## Getting Started

### Create Your Account
1. Open the app on your phone or computer
2. Enter your email and password
3. Set your display name (how friends will see you in the trip)
4. You're ready to go!

### Create a Trip
1. Tap **"+ Create Trip"**
2. Give your trip a name (e.g., "Summer in Japan")
3. Add the start and end dates
4. Optional: Add a description or currency
5. Invite friends by sharing the Trip ID with them

### Invite Friends
1. Go to trip settings
2. Share the **Trip ID** with your friends
3. They can join using **"+ Join Trip"** and entering the ID

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I use this with friends who don't have the app?**
A: No, everyone needs to create an account. But it takes less than a minute!

**Q: What if someone leaves the trip?**
A: Their expenses and balance stay on record. You can manually track settling up with them.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes! Only people you invite can see your trips.

**Q: Can I edit trips after they end?**
A: Yes! Your trip data is always there to look back on.

**Q: What if I forget the Trip ID?**
A: Check your trip's settings - the ID is always displayed there.

## Need Help?

If something isn't working as expected:
